Sadza

Instructions

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

4 Servings

Ingredients and Utensils

  • 1 cup cold water
  • 750 ml boiling water
  • 7 scoops mealie-meal
  • Wooden spoon

To do

  • Firstly wash your hands.
  • Add mealie-meal into a pot. Add cold water, and stir to make a paste.
  • Place pot on the stove and add boiling water stirring simultaneously to avoid lumps
  • Keep stirring until its boiling (rakukwata). Cover the pot and reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15 minutes
  • After 15 minutes, add more mealie-meal bit by bit and mix the sadza (kumona sadza) with a wooden sppon. When its just about to reach the consistency you want, cover it and let it simmer (rishinyire) for 5 minutes
  • Your sadza is ready and can be served with your preferred relish.
  • Enjoy!
