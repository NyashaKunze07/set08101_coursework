Mupunga une dovi ~ Peanut Butter Rice

Intructions

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Servings 4-5

Ingredients and Utensils

  • 3 cups rice
  • 6 cups water + extra for when mixing
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 5 tbsp smooth peanut butter
  • medium sized pot
  • Wooden spoon
  • Tablespoon

To do

  • Wash your hands firstly.
  • Place rice in the pot. Add water , add salt and bring to a rapid boil then reduce the heat
  • As the water reduces in the pot, continue to gradually reduce the heat
  • Add the peanut butter in batches, together with a little bit of hot water and mix
  • Enjoy!
