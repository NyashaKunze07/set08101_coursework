Peanut Butter Porridge

Instructions

Preparation Time:20 minutes

Servings 4

Ingredients and Utensils

  • 1 1/2 mealie-meal
  • 60ml warm water
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 pot
  • 1 wooden spoon/ whisk (musika)
To do
  • Get your ingredients together
  • Put your hupfu (mealie-meal) in a pot, add warm water to make a paste. Add boiling water whilst stirring simultaneously and bring it to the boil.
  • As soon as it begins to boil, reduce heat, close pot and let it simmer for 15 minutes
  • If you feel it's too thick, you may add more boiling water until you get the consistency you like
  • After 15 minutes, add salt and sugar then stir. Your porridge is ready.
  • You may add lemon juice, margarine, peanut butter or roughly broken up pieces of bread.
  • Enjoy!
