Muboora - Pumpkin Leaves

Instructions

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Servings 3

Ingredients and Utensils

  • 1 bundle muboora (pumpkin leaves
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp pure vegetable oil
  • 500ml boiling water
  • 2 tomatoes(chopped)
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)

To do

  • Wash your hands and get your ingredients together
  • Wash your pumpkin leaves. Given them a thorough wash until satisfied that it's clean.
  • Break of part of the stem and pull of the silk from the pumpkin leaves. Do this one leaf at a time. In Shona it's called this kufurura.
  • After kufurura, cut your pumpkin leaves up. Put some water(500ml boiling water) in a pot and add bicarbonate of soda. Close the pot and bring the water to a boil.
  • Add your cut up pumpkin leaves to the boiling water and close the pot. Bring to the boil for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally in between. After 5 minutes drain your pumpkin leaves in a colander.
  • Give your pot a quick rinse (not the pumpkin leaves) then return the pumpkin leaves to the pot. Add cooking oil, salt, onions and tomatoes. Stir, reduce heat, cose pot and let simmer until tomatoes and onions are cooked through (about 10 minutes)
  • The pumpkin leaves are done. and can be served with sadza
  • Enjoy!
