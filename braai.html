Braai

Instructions

Preparation Time: 1hr

Serves 1-2

Ingredients and Utensils

  • 1 Red meat,preferably rump, fillet or sirloin
  • 1 Balsamic vinegar
  • Spices of your choice
  • 1 salt
  • 1 pepper
  • 1 Olive oil
  • 1 braai stand / grill
  • Charcoal/ wood/ briquettes

To do

  • Firstly wash your hands and then your meat.
  • Place meat on a clean surface.
  • Gently rub olive oil, the spices, salt and pepper on both sides of the meat.
  • Leaving the meat for 20 minutes.
  • Prepare the braai stand/grill with fire using charcoal/ wood/ briquettes.
  • Wait until coals or grey ash has formed in your fire, then your fire is ready for braai.
  • Before placing meat on the grill, rub the meat with oil to maintain the juicyness of it on the grill.
  • Avoid turning the meat too often as it becomes too dry. For a well-done braai, keep the meat on the grill a bit longer.
  • Once meat is all cooked, take it off grill and let it rest fro 5 minutes before serving
  • Serve it while warm, with sadza or bread rolls and a nice green salad of your choice
  • Enjoy!
