Okra - Derere

Instructions

Preparation Time: 18 minutes

Servings : 4

Ingredients and Utensils

  • 500g okra
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 medium tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 300ml boiling water

To do

  • Wash and cut your okra into rings. Chop your onion and tomato
  • Put boiling water into a pot, add bicarbonate of soda and bring this water to the boil.
  • As soon as the water boils, add your okra and onions.
  • Whisk or stir briskly for about 3 minutes. Add tomatoes and continue stirring until the tomatoes are cooked through (give 3 minutes) If adding chili pepper , this would be the perfect time to add it.
  • Season with salt and ground black pepper. Simmer for the final 2 minutes and enjoyy!
