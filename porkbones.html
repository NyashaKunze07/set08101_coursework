Pork Bones- Zimbabwean Recipe

Instructions

Preparation Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Serves 4

Ingredients and Utensils

  • 1 kg pork bones
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Enough chicken stock to cover the bones
  • 50 - 100ml of water
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 3 medium tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1/2 medium onion

To do

  • Firstly wash your hands. Yes you!
  • Soak your kapenta in boiling water for 15 minutes. Make sure they are all immersed in the water.
  • After 15 minutes, drain and pat dry your kapenta. Heat oil in a pan and add the kapenta
  • Fry until brown, then add onions and curry powder. Stir and fry the onion and kapenta for about a minute.
  • Add Tomatoes, stir and reduce heat and cover the pan for about 5 minutes to allow the tomatoes to get cooked.
  • After 5 minutes, add a little water and soup powder if one wants to make a stew. Cover pot for 3 minutes and the kapenta stew is ready to serve.
  • Serve it while warm, with sadza
  • Enjoy!
