Pork Bones- Zimbabwean Recipe

Instructions

Preparation Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Serves 4

Ingredients and Utensils

  • 1 kg pork bones
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Enough chicken stock to cover the bones
  • 50 - 100ml of water
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 3 medium tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1/2 medium onion

To do

  • Firstly wash your hands. Yes you!
  • Heat oil in a pot and add the pork bones and brown them
  • Once browned, add the chicken stock ,garlic and 1 tsp salt. Bring to the boil then reduce heat to a gentle simmer.
  • Simmer until the meat on the bones is tender and the water is finished
  • Add onions, curry powder. Fry for 2 minutes. add tomatoes, tomatoe paste ,paprika and black pepper and water. Stir and allow to cook for 3-5 minutes
  • This can be enjoyed with sadza or potatoes.
  • Enjoy!
