Maguru -Tripe

Maguru (tripe) is one of Zimbabwe's most popular traditional dishes. Zimbabweans, especially those who live in rural areas, always look forward to maguru whenever a beast (cow) is slaughtered. This treat tastes fantastic when served with sadza. You can find the recipe here: Tripe Recipe.

Kapenta

Kapenta , known as matemba in Shona, are one of Zimbabwe's traditional meals. Many Zimbabwean families eat this. You can find the recipe for this delicious treat here: Kapenta Recipe

Haifiridzi

Beef Haifiridzi is a popular dish many Zimbabweans grew up eating. This dish takes its name haifiridzi from Highfield, one of Zimbabwe's oldest suburbs in the its capital Harare. Houses around Highfield and many other surburs across Zimbabwe usually have a small garden in the backyard growing leafy greens. Taking the leafy greens and adding to the meat instantly beefs up your dish allowing you to feed many. You can find the recipe here : Haifiridzi Recipe.
