Tripe ~ Maguru stew

Instructions

Preparation Time: 7hours 45 minutes

Serves 4

Ingredients and Utensils

  • 500g tripe (maguru)
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)
  • 1 and 1/2 tomatoes(chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger (minced)
  • 1 tbs soup powder
  • 1 litre water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • Cooking oil

To do

  • NB : it is important to thorougly wash the tripe in warm water and trim off any excess fat to avoid having any fat emulsifying(kugwamba) in the pot or in the plate
  • After washing your washing your tripe and trimming off excess fat, cut into good size pieces and place into pot. Add water and salt. Partly cover the pot and bring to boil
  • Once boiled reduce heat to the lowest mark and completely cover pot living into to simaer for 7.5 hours or over night.
  • After the water is finished, add garlic, ginger, add frying oil and stir. Add spices of your choice and stir, then add tomatoes and stir
  • Add the tomato sauce and stir. Allow the tomatoes to be completely cooked (2 minutes). After, add soup powder and pour cold water directly into soup powder. Cover pot and let it simmer for 5 minutes
  • After 5 minutes, your maguru are done.
  • Taste for seasoning and adjust accordingly.
  • Enjoy!
